Privacy Policy

TL;DR: Habitori does not collect, share, or transmit your personal information. Your data stays on your device. Optional iCloud sync stores data in your personal iCloud account only.

1. Introduction

Habitori ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Habitori for iOS, iPadOS, and watchOS.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Habitori operates entirely on your device and does not send any data to our servers. The app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

3. Data You Create

When you use Habitori, the following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Habits: Names, descriptions, colors, notes, reminders, and scheduling preferences
  • Completions: Dates and times when you complete habits, optional notes and mood ratings
  • Tasks: Task lists associated with your habits
  • Stages: Milestone information and progress tracking
  • Dismissals: Records of when you dismiss habits for specific days or weeks
  • Earned Badges: Achievement records including the date earned and associated habit
  • Settings: Your app preferences and configuration

All of this data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework in a local SQLite database on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: By default, all your data is stored locally on your iOS/iPadOS device in the app's secure container. On watchOS, data is stored in the Watch app's container and synced via Bluetooth to your iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework.

App Group Storage: To enable data sharing between the iOS app and Apple Watch app, we use an App Group container (group.dev.northwake.HabitKit). This is a secure iOS feature that allows apps from the same developer to share data on your device only.

Optional iCloud Sync: You may optionally enable iCloud sync in the app settings. When enabled:

  • Your habit data is synced to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's security standards
  • Only you can access this data across your Apple devices signed in to the same iCloud account
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

5. Permissions

Habitori may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you habit reminders at scheduled times. You can customize notification settings in the app.
  • iCloud: Only requested if you enable iCloud sync. Used to sync your data across your devices.

All permissions are optional. The app will function without them, though some features may be limited.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.

Habitori does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. Your data stays entirely on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

7. Data Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export your habit data at any time from the app's settings in JSON format for backup purposes. The exported file includes all your habits, tasks, completions, dismissals, stages, and earned badges.
  • Import: You can import previously exported data back into the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual habits or all your data at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can delete iCloud data by disabling sync and deleting the data from your device, or by managing your iCloud storage in iOS Settings.

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data will remain in your iCloud account even if you uninstall the app from a device, allowing you to restore it when you reinstall the app on any device signed in to the same iCloud account.

You can delete your data at any time through the app's settings, or by uninstalling the app (which deletes local data only). To delete iCloud data, disable iCloud sync in the app settings and then manage your iCloud storage through iOS Settings.

9. Third-Party Services

Habitori does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. The only Apple services used are:

  • SwiftData/CoreData: For local data persistence
  • CloudKit: For optional iCloud sync (only if you enable it)
  • UserNotifications: For local notifications
  • WatchConnectivity: For Apple Watch sync via Bluetooth

10. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can access your data at any time by exporting it from the app's settings
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete your data at any time through the app's settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data in JSON format for use in other services
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

To exercise any of these rights, use the features provided in the app or contact us using the information provided below.

11. Children's Privacy

Habitori is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is rated 17+ due to references to substance recovery support in habit templates.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
